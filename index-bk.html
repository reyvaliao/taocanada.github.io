Who we are

About TAO Canada

TAO Canada was born out of a shared vision and a commitment to excellence. Founded by four passionate members of the Tau Gamma Phi Fraternity, our journey began in the vibrant city of Toronto. We believe that together, we can achieve greatness and make a positive impact on our community.

Our Mission

At TAO Canada, our mission is twofold:

  • Fostering Excellence: We strive to create a supportive and empowering community for members of Tau Gamma Phi in Canada. Whether you’re a student, a professional, or an entrepreneur, we’re here to help you excel in your chosen field. Through mentorship, networking, and skill-building, we aim to elevate each other to new heights.
  • Serving Our Community: We recognize the importance of giving back. TAO Canada is committed to making a difference in our local community. Through charitable initiatives, volunteer work, and community outreach, we aim to be a force for good. From organizing food drives to participating in environmental cleanups, we’re dedicated to creating positive change.

Our Values

  • Unity: We stand united as brothers and sisters, transcending boundaries and backgrounds. Together, we are stronger.
  • Excellence: We believe in continuous growth and the pursuit of excellence. Whether it’s academic achievements, professional success, or personal development, we encourage each other to reach for the stars.
  • Compassion: Our hearts beat for our community. We extend a helping hand to those in need, fostering compassion and empathy.

Are you a TRISKELION?

Are you ready to be part of an extraordinary community? Look no further! Triskelion welcomes you with open arms. As a member, you’ll unlock a world of opportunities, connections, and shared experiences.

Triskelion Alumni Organization of Canada Vision

TAO Canada is dedicated to helping brothers and Sisters to be the godly men, God calls them to be. Where a godly man is define as a servant, not a slave-master.
